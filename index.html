We generate Hyper-Immersive Panoramic scenes

from a single text-description

Abstract

3D immersive scene generation is a topic of growing interest. Yet, the scarcity of available 3D training data and the complexity of representing 3D scenes efficiently make this a significantly more challenging problem than 2D image generation. To address this challenge, we introduce HyperPano---a framework for immersive panoramic 3D scene generation from a single text prompt. HyperPano makes several important design choices: 1) we argue that panoramas are a powerful prior to represent immersive 2D scenes and lift them into a 3D Gaussian representation to splat detailed 360-degree omnidirectional scenes; 2) we leverage the advantages of both perspective images and panoramas captured by two different camera projection models. Our unique multi-view diffusion and panorama outpainting models are combined to generate a high-resolution panorama. Extensive experiments demonstrate that our framework achieves state-of-the-art performance in both quality and diversity. We believe that HyperPano holds promise for advancing 3D panoramic scene creation with numerous applications.

Method Overview

Overview of pipeline. Our generation pipeline consists of two stages. In the panorama generation stage, we focus on synthesizing a high-quality panoramic image with multi-view diffusion, panorama outpainting model and SR module. In the panoramic-3D-Gaussian-scene-construction stage, We optimize and fine-tune a 3D Gaussian panoramic scene from high-quality panorama for the better user immersive experience.
